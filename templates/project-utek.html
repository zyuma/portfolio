City of Vancouver Earthquake Relief
Jan 21 2012

The City of Vancouver Earthquake Relief was the theme for the Consulting category of University of Toronto Engineering Kompetition(UTEK) 2012. We worked in a team of four to design and present our solution to help minimize the City of Vancouver's vulnerability against earthquakes. Our solution was in the form of a protocol which address the actions to be taken by the city and its civilians during the event of an earthquake. This solution won the Most Creative Design award.
