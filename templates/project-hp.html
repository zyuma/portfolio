Meeting Task Organizer App
Oct 12-13 2013

This mobile app was developed for the HP Anywhere enterprise at the HP Anywhere Hackathon event in San Francisco. The title of the app is Meeting Task Organizer, which was designed to act as a visual aid tool for the assignments of tasks during meetings. It features a 'drag and drop' of pre-inputted list of tasks into the to-do lists of each of the participants of the meeting. The movement of the tasks is saved in a database for each participants to periodically fetch for the screen sharing effect. Front end development was done in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Backend development was done in Java.
